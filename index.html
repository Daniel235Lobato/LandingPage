Nestled within the scenic landscapes of Magna, Utah, Little Valley is a community where modern living meets a classic neighborhood feel. With the Oquirrh Mountains as our backdrop, we pride ourselves on being a tight-knit, family-oriented enclave dedicated to maintaining a safe and beautiful environment for all residents. Whether we're coordinating home improvement projects or sharing updates through the Owners Association, our goal is simple: to keep Little Valley the best place to call home.
